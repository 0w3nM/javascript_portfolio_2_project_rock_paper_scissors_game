Game Rules

Rules:
- The User has the choice of 5 cards.
- Every time a card is picked the computer picks a card at random.
- The Results can either be, Win Lose or Draw.
- The User gets 5 turns and MUST win 3 times out of the 5.


Winning and Losing choices:

- Rock beats Scissors and Lizard

- Paper beats Rock and Spock

- Scissors beats Paper and Lizard

- Spock beats Rock and Scissors

- Lizard beats Spock and Paper
